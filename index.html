About Vocaxion

Vocaxion is an innovative early-stage LLC dedicated to revolutionizing career training through interactive gaming experiences. We believe that learning should be engaging, practical, and directly applicable to real-world professional scenarios.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional education and practical career skills by creating immersive, game-based training programs. Starting with the craft industries, we're developing comprehensive modules that train individuals for successful careers in skilled trades and artisan professions.

Founded by experienced instructional designers with expertise across construction, accounting, technology, and various other fields, Vocaxion combines deep industry knowledge with cutting-edge educational technology to create training experiences that are both effective and enjoyable.
